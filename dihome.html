    Website Development in wordpress with plugin integration according to client specifics.

    The problem

    Dihome interiores, located in the heart of Oporto City, sought to transform its outdated website and social media presence. The primary challenge was to create a serene and cohesive design that resonated with both business and residential clients.

    Social Media Campaign

    Our strategy focused on creating visually stunning content that showcased their unique design furniture, including sizes and description. We leveraged platforms like Instagram, LinkedIn, and Facebook, using a mix of high-quality images, videos, and stories.

    Users & Audience

    The target users of Dihome Website are Hospitality and Architecture professionals working in the Residential market.

    Key pain points

    Translate the old website content to a more modern and user friendly experience.


    The Results

    The new Dihome website successfully transformed the online user experience, making it easier for clients to explore and find the company’s portfolio. The visually engaging design and improved functionality led to a significant increase in web traffic and business contacts. Enhanced SEO strategies resulted in better search engine rankings, driving more organic traffic to the site.
