Coffee Break

Coffee Break is an app concept that promotes team building events in a fun and engaging way by enabling co-workers to see and compare their achievements and interact with each other.

The problem

How can a start-up company retain a strong sense of community in the face of expansion and how can we help newcomers connect with the team?

Users & Audience

The target users of coffee break are young professionals working in the creative industries.

Key pain points

Easier way to share their achievements and to Keep up-dated of everyone’s accomplishments.

User Interface Design

In order to make the app fun and unique I used a minimal approach with rounded corners, vibrant gradients, big beautiful photos, and simple, clean icons.
