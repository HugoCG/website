P #01

Image+Photoshop

The process to attain the final image is achieved throught physical handling of the printed photograph in contrast to what happens in digital edition. In this sequence there is no illusion that we are facing a real image. The operations of cut, paste and copy are present in the final result as can be verified by observing the thickness of the layers that constitute the image.

Exploration of the barriers between the virtual and physical space, image processing without the use of the digital edition requires to operate within the visible realm of texture. Different layers of information are overlapped in order to create a new image.
